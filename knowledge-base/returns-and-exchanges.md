# Returns & Exchanges — Trailhead Outfitters

## The short version
- **30-day return window** from the delivery date.
- Items must be **unworn, unused, and have original tags** attached.
- **Free return shipping** — we email you a prepaid label.
- **Exchanges** for a different size or color are free (subject to stock).
- **Final-sale / clearance items can't be returned or exchanged.**

## What can be returned
Eligible items are those that are:
1. Delivered **within the last 30 days**, and
2. **Unworn and unused**, with original tags and packaging, and
3. **Not marked final sale** (clearance items show "Final sale" on the product page and order).

Lightly trying something on indoors is fine — items that have been worn outdoors, washed, or
damaged through use are not eligible for a standard return.

## How to start a return or exchange
1. In chat, choose **"Returns & exchanges"** and enter your order number (**TO-#####**).
2. We check eligibility automatically and confirm.
3. Choose **return for refund** or **exchange** (and the new size/color for exchanges).
4. We email a **prepaid return label** and return instructions. Pack the item with its tags and
   drop it off with the carrier.

## Refunds
- Refunds are issued to your **original payment method** once we receive and inspect the item.
- Inspection + refund typically takes **3–5 business days** after the item arrives at our
  warehouse; your bank may take a few additional days to post it.
- Original shipping charges (if any) are **non-refundable**, but return shipping is free.

## Exchanges
- Exchanges are for a **different size or color of the same item**.
- If the size/color you want is **out of stock**, we'll offer to notify you, suggest an
  alternative, or process a refund instead.
- Want a **different product**? Return the original for a refund and place a new order.

## Final-sale items
- Items marked **"Final sale"** (clearance, deeply discounted) are **not returnable or
  exchangeable**. This is noted on the product page and your order confirmation.

## Defective or damaged items
- If an item has a **manufacturing defect** or **arrived damaged**, we'll make it right even
  outside the 30-day window — this is handled under our warranty, not the standard return
  policy. See **Warranty & Care**, or ask to **talk to a human** and we'll take care of it.

## Gifts
- Received something as a gift? We can set up a return for store credit. Because gift orders
  are under the purchaser's account, a support agent will help — just ask to talk to a human.
