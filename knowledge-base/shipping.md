# Shipping & Delivery — Trailhead Outfitters

## Shipping rates
- **Free standard shipping on orders over $75** (after discounts, before tax).
- Orders **under $75**: flat **$6.95** standard shipping.
- **Expedited shipping**: flat **$16.95**, available at checkout.

## Processing & delivery times
- Orders are **processed within 1 business day** (Mon–Fri). Orders placed on weekends or
  holidays begin processing the next business day.
- **Standard delivery: 5–7 business days** after the order ships.
- **Expedited delivery: 2–3 business days** after the order ships.
- You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order leaves
  our warehouse.

## Where we ship
- We ship anywhere in the **United States**, including **Alaska, Hawaii, P.O. boxes, and
  APO/FPO** addresses (standard shipping only; allow extra time).
- We also ship to **Canada**. Canadian orders may be subject to **import duties and taxes**
  collected by the carrier at delivery.
- We do **not** currently ship to other international destinations.

## Tracking your order
- Use the **"Track my order"** option in chat with your order number (format **TO-#####**) for
  the latest status.
- You can also click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

## Split shipments
- Larger orders may ship in **more than one parcel**, sometimes on different days. Each parcel
  gets its own tracking number, and you're only charged shipping once.

## Delayed, lost, or damaged in transit
- Carriers occasionally report delays. If your estimated delivery date has passed by more than
  **2 business days**, contact us and we'll investigate or reship.
- If a package is marked delivered but you can't find it, check around your property and with
  neighbors first, then contact us within **7 days** so we can help.
- If an item **arrives damaged**, keep the packaging and reach out right away — we'll send a
  replacement or refund. (See Warranty & Care.)

## Changing your shipping address
- If your order hasn't shipped yet (status **Processing**), we can usually update the address —
  contact us as soon as possible. Once an order has shipped, the address can't be changed.
