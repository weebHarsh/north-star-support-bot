# Store Info & FAQ — Trailhead Outfitters

## About us
**Trailhead Outfitters** is a small online shop for hikers and campers. We sell outdoor apparel
(jackets, base layers, hiking pants, socks) and camping gear (tents, sleeping bags, backpacks,
stoves, headlamps, water filters) — practical, well-made kit for 3-season trips and beyond.

## Customer support hours
- **Live support: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM ET.**
- Our **chatbot is available 24/7** for tracking, returns, recommendations, and FAQs.
- Outside live hours, you can still start a request in chat and we'll **follow up by email,
  usually within one business day**.

## Contact
- **Chat:** right here (fastest for orders, returns, and recommendations).
- **Email:** support@trailheadoutfitters.example
- **Phone:** 1-800-555-TRAIL (1-800-555-8724), Mon–Fri 9–6 ET.

## Payment methods
We accept **Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover**, plus **Apple Pay, Google Pay,
PayPal, and Shop Pay**. Payment is charged when your order ships.

## Order changes & cancellations
- Need to change or cancel an order? If it's still **Processing** (not yet shipped), we can
  usually help — contact us quickly.
- Once an order has **shipped**, it can't be changed or cancelled, but you can return eligible
  items within 30 days of delivery.

## Account & orders
- You don't need an account to order or to track an order in chat — your **order number
  (TO-#####)** is enough.
- For privacy, we may ask for the **email on the order** before sharing order details.

## Gift cards
- Digital gift cards are available in $25–$200 denominations and never expire. Gift cards are
  non-refundable.

## Price adjustments
- If an item you bought goes on sale within **7 days** of your purchase, we'll refund the
  difference — just ask. (Excludes final-sale/clearance items.)

## Sustainability
- We use recycled materials where we can (like our Cedar Fleece) and offer repairs under
  warranty to keep gear on the trail and out of landfills.

## Common questions
- **Do you ship to Canada?** Yes — duties/taxes may apply at delivery. We don't ship elsewhere
  internationally yet. (See Shipping.)
- **How long do refunds take?** 3–5 business days after we receive the item, plus bank posting
  time. (See Returns & Exchanges.)
- **Is my item returnable?** Within 30 days of delivery, unworn with tags, non-final-sale.
  (See Returns & Exchanges.)
- **What if my item is defective?** Covered by our 1-year warranty, even past 30 days.
  (See Warranty & Care.)
